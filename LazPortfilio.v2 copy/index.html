About Myself

On June 4th, 2017 I decided that I wanted to learn how to code. I was reading The 10x Rule by Grant Cardone and something he said sparked a very intense desire to put my ideas out into the world. The funny thing about my decision to learn to code is that I have always said “I am not very techy.” For the longest time I didn’t even know how to recover my Facebook login. Needless to say, that has all drastically changed. I have spent the last 3 months on a relentless campaign to teach myself the art of web/ app development. I was spending anywhere between 5-7 hrs/ day taking classes on Codecademy.com, freeCodeCamp.com, TeamTreeHouse.com, Udemy and pretty much anything I could get my hands on. I have learned Front end development and back end development. I have become efficient in HTML, CSS, javascript, and much more. So at this point, I am passionate to continue my learning and become a part of an amazing community of like minded programers who want to help people solve problems.
